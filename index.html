License Revocation Policy

Revocation refers to the withdrawal of a user's access to courses or programs provided under DLSEI programme with certain conditions applied.

Conditions for License Expiration:

  1. If a learner does not accept the Coursera license invitation and enroll in at least one course within 15 days of receiving it, the license will be revoked.
  2. To retain the license, learners must complete at least one individual course within 30 days of receiving the license invitation.

Note: This includes any short course or guided project available on the platform.
